Years Of Experience

Effs stands out for the experience and expertise of its professional team. EFSS was established in 2015 as the sole distributor for two matica companies in Greater Cairo and industrial areas, and then expanded its activity by providing a comprehensive security system for clubs, universities, companies and institutions. One year later, EFSS established a streamlined card development department and demonstrated rapid and successful growth. By focusing on market needs and quickly responding to those needs through customization solutions, EFSS has completed its growth by exporting its systems to some Arab countries. EFSS always maintains its relationships with its customers and their loyalty through technical support and strong customer feedback. EFSS specializes in access control, attendance, security systems and smart solutions through smart cards.
